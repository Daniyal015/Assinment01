Recipe of the Day: Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classical italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper . Its simple, delicious, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water untill at dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with grated cheeses.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix with pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the egg and cheese mixture.
  6. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
